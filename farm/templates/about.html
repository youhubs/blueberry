About Us

Welcome to our farm! We are a family-owned business that specializes in growing high-quality fruits and vegetables in our greenhouses and fields. We take pride in using sustainable farming practices to produce healthy, delicious produce for our customers.

In addition to our fresh produce, we also offer a variety of value-added products such as jams, jellies, and baked goods made from our farm-fresh ingredients. We believe in supporting our local community and source many of our ingredients from other local farmers and producers.

Our picking events are a fun way for families and friends to experience the farm and pick their own fresh produce. We offer picking events in the spring and fall when our blueberries, strawberries, and other fruits and vegetables are in season.

Thank you for visiting our website and supporting our farm. We look forward to seeing you soon!
